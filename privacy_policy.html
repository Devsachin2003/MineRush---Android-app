Privacy Policy for MineRush

Introduction

Welcome to MineRush. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide while using our app designed to explore coal mining rules, acts, and opportunities.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, organization details (if applicable), and login credentials.
  • Interaction Data: Queries entered, pages visited, and features accessed (chatbot, internship finder, glossary, etc.).
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, device identifiers for analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize the information shown about coal mining acts and policies.
  • To improve the performance and reliability of chatbot and search features.
  • To help users find internship opportunities in the mining industry.
  • To perform user behavior analysis and enhance app usability.

Data Security

  • All user data is stored securely with industry-standard encryption methods.
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • We regularly review our data protection practices to stay up to date with best practices.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may only share information:

  • With government authorities when legally required.
  • With third parties only after obtaining your explicit consent for collaboration or research purposes.

Your Rights

  • You can view and update your profile information within the app.
  • You may request account deletion at any time through the delete account option.
  • You can revoke permissions previously granted, such as location or usage tracking.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

MineRush may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any major changes will be communicated through app updates or email notifications.
